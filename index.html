About me

I am a musician and programmer.
Recently finished a web dev bootcamp at Ironhack.
With experience in programming in JavaScript and game development with Unity Engine and C#. My goal is to keep learning in a healthy environment with a good team.
I have always worked at customer services and IT related jobs. Seeking always to be creative and looking for new ways to learn.
During the lockout in 2020 I started a few basic web dev courses on Udemy and I realized that was one of the best things I've ever learned. I've never realized I could create a whole app or webpage with that much functionality starting from scratch. That bit of knowledge helped me to work for a few months in a company developing an Alexa skill.
In 2021, I decided to start a few video tutorials on YouTube to share with the rest of the community my game development learning process and the results.
In 2022 I joined a bootcamp for an intensive 9-week web dev course in order to improve my dev skills and to opt for a new job.
